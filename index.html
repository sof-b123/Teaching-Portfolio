Austins Butterfly

    Summary of video
    * Assigned task of drawing a butterfly scientifically to year 1 student, Austin.
    * Initial drawing showed basic understanding but lacked detail and accuracy.
    * Provided specific, auctionable feedback to Austin, focusing on imporvind wing structure, body parts and placement.
    * Encouraged Austin to iterate on his drawing based on the feedback recieved.
    * Huided the iterative process by continually reviewing and providing constructive criticism.
    * Witnessed Austin's growth as each revision showed significant improvement in detail and accuracy.
    * Demonstrated the effectiveness of connstructive feedback in fostering learning and skill development.
    The stories importance
    * This story vividly demonstrates the importance of feedback and assistance and group work as with correct wording and constructive feedback, growth of students can occur
    * This story supports a pedagogical approach that values process over immideate results, promoting a growth mindset and fostering a culture of learning
    * This story also educates teachers that it is okay to criticise students work in the correct manner as it may further develop their skills and students development
